About Enersave Lighting Solutions

Proud to have been in the forefront of providing Energy Saving Lighting Solutions to our vast base of clients. Every day we help customers discover more and more benefits of Energy Conserving, eco friendly way of life.

By presenting a high quality and yet affordable Lighting Solutions package, Enersave has today earned and retained the trust of its prestigious clientele. Enersave always is trying to bring in latest lighting products to the easy reach of its clients along with the technical details to assist in achieving the right illumination levels required.

A fully functional live demo centre at our office with hundreds of lighting products, on display gives all our customers and visitors a chance to experience the lighting comforts of different fixtures. Enersave regularly assists industry professionals in getting the right lighting climate in many of their prestigious projects.

With absolute emphasis on a fully computerized ERP system to monitor its performance and progress at every stage, Enersave has been able to reduce lead time for supplies and ensure near perfect delivery schedules to projects. Enersave has its own processing facility which ensures quality components are used for long lasting trouble free performace.

Enersave welcomes you to experience a completely different way of lighting up your projects and residences.
